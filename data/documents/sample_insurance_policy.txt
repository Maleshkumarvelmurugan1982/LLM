# Sample Insurance Policy Document

## HEALTH INSURANCE POLICY

### Policy Number: HIP-2024-001
### Effective Date: January 1, 2024

## SECTION 1: COVERAGE OVERVIEW

This health insurance policy provides comprehensive medical coverage for policyholders and their dependents. Coverage includes hospitalization, surgical procedures, diagnostic tests, and preventive care.

### 1.1 Covered Medical Procedures

The following medical procedures are covered under this policy:

#### Orthopedic Procedures
- **Knee Surgery**: Including knee replacement, arthroscopy, and repair procedures
  - Coverage: 80% of eligible expenses
  - Maximum benefit: ₹2,00,000 per policy year
  - Waiting period: 90 days from policy inception

- **Hip Surgery**: Including hip replacement and repair procedures  
  - Coverage: 85% of eligible expenses
  - Maximum benefit: ₹2,50,000 per policy year
  - Waiting period: 90 days from policy inception

- **Shoulder Surgery**: Including rotator cuff repair and joint replacement
  - Coverage: 75% of eligible expenses
  - Maximum benefit: ₹1,50,000 per policy year
  - Waiting period: 90 days from policy inception

#### Cardiac Procedures
- **Heart Surgery**: Including bypass surgery, valve replacement, and angioplasty
  - Coverage: 90% of eligible expenses
  - Maximum benefit: ₹5,00,000 per policy year
  - Waiting period: 180 days from policy inception

- **Cardiac Catheterization**: Diagnostic and therapeutic procedures
  - Coverage: 85% of eligible expenses
  - Maximum benefit: ₹1,00,000 per policy year
  - Waiting period: 30 days from policy inception

### 1.2 Geographic Coverage

Medical treatments are covered when performed at:
- Network hospitals within India
- Emergency treatment anywhere in India
- Pre-approved treatment at international facilities (subject to conditions)

Covered cities include but are not limited to:
- Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Chennai, Kolkata
- Pune, Hyderabad, Ahmedabad, Jaipur, Lucknow
- All Tier-1 and Tier-2 cities with network hospitals

---

## SECTION 2: ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA

### 2.1 Age Requirements
- Minimum entry age: 18 years
- Maximum entry age: 65 years
- Coverage continuation up to age 80

### 2.2 Policy Waiting Periods

#### General Waiting Period
All non-emergency medical treatments require a minimum waiting period of 30 days from policy inception date.

#### Specific Waiting Periods
- **Major Surgeries**: 90 days waiting period
- **Pre-existing Conditions**: 24 months waiting period
- **Maternity Coverage**: 10 months waiting period

### 2.3 Coverage Limitations

Maximum coverage amounts per policy year:
- Individual coverage: ₹5,00,000
- Family floater: ₹10,00,000
- Critical illness: ₹25,00,000

---

## SECTION 3: CLAIMS PROCESS

### 3.1 Claim Filing Requirements

To file a claim, policyholders must:
1. Notify the insurance company within 24 hours of admission
2. Submit all required documents within 30 days
3. Provide original medical bills and discharge summary
4. Complete the claim form with accurate information

### 3.2 Required Documents

- Policy certificate
- Medical bills and receipts
- Hospital discharge summary
- Doctor's treatment certificate
- Diagnostic test reports
- Identity proof and age proof

### 3.3 Claim Settlement Timeline

- **Cashless Claims**: Pre-authorization within 2 hours
- **Reimbursement Claims**: Settlement within 15 working days
- **Complex Cases**: May require up to 30 days for investigation

---

## SECTION 4: EXCLUSIONS

### 4.1 General Exclusions

The following are not covered under this policy:

#### Cosmetic Procedures
- Plastic surgery for aesthetic purposes
- Cosmetic dental procedures
- Hair transplantation
- Skin treatments for beauty enhancement

#### Experimental Treatments
- Investigational drugs or procedures
- Clinical trial participation
- Unproven medical treatments
- Alternative medicine not recognized by medical boards

#### Lifestyle-Related Exclusions
- Treatment for substance abuse
- Self-inflicted injuries
- Injuries during illegal activities
- War and terrorism-related injuries

### 4.2 Specific Medical Exclusions

- Congenital disorders (unless covered after waiting period)
- Mental health conditions (limited coverage available)
- Dental treatment (except accidental damage)
- Vision correction procedures

---

## SECTION 5: POLICY CONDITIONS

### 5.1 Premium Payment

- Annual premium payment required
- Grace period: 30 days from due date
- Policy lapses if premium not paid within grace period
- Reinstatement possible with medical underwriting

### 5.2 Policy Renewal

- Guaranteed renewal up to age 80
- Premium may increase based on age and claims experience
- No waiting period for renewed policies
- Continuous coverage benefits maintained

### 5.3 Network Hospitals

Treatment at network hospitals provides:
- Cashless treatment facility
- Direct billing arrangement
- Quality assured medical care
- 24/7 customer support

Current network includes over 5,000 hospitals across India.
